these are a few artists who inspire me :)

by Cianna Price

I am inspired by:

Kehinde Wiley

This artist inspires me through the message he puts out with hispaintings & exhibitions.
Wiley’s work is known for placing its subject, African-Americans of the contemporary era, in traditionally classical European spaces.

His work uplifts the beauty and power of the average Black American, while tearing down stereotypes commonly held by artists of the past. I also take a lot of inspiration from his use of realism and saturated color palettes.

I am inspired by:

Frank Ocean


Another artist I take inspiration from. I’ve been a fan since 2018, and his musical & lyrical skill have helped me throughout highschool.

His storytelling and melodic/moody tune is something I continue enjoying up to this day. A personal favorite of mine is Seigfried, a song which discusses themes of self-comparison and of self-reflection.

I am inspired by:

Alejandro Sanchéz Rodríguez



Jorge Jimenez



A third artist I admire more for their technical skill and continuity in style. Alejandro Sanchéz Rodríguez is a colorist for both DC and Marvel comics; his method of color picking and blending have really inspired me to further improve my own coloring in digital art.

My favorite works of his are those done in collaboration with Jorge Jimenez, another reputable comic book artist for DC/Warner Bros. I often look to both artists as I try to improve my skills in digital art.
